Welcome to my WORLD

I'm on a journey to create value through finding joy in whatever I do. Here, I'll be sharing some of the stories from that journey, which has so far taken me across 24 countries and 78 cities. But this is far from a travel blog. That's the same as if I were to say it's a house blog, because I'm writing it from home. The places are simply the context for the "adventure" - which is actually just another word for being outside of one's comfort zone - the place where the most learning and growth happens. It just so happens that it's easiest to be outside of your comfort zone when you're in a place where you know nobody, and even better if those people whom you don't know don't speak your language.
